Simon's Rules

  1. Press Start to start the game
  2. You are at Level 1, Simon will give you a clue color to choose, for example: Red
  3. Choose the color Simon has given, choose Red
  4. You are at Level 2, Simon will give you a clue again, for example: Yellow
  5. You have to remember color at level 1, so you have to choose Red followed by Yellow
  6. You are at level 3, Simon will give you a clue again, for example: Green
  7. You have to remember color at level 1 and level 2, so you have to choose Red then Yellow and then Green
  8. And so on...
